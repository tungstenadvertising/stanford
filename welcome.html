Stanford Medicine MedAdmit Portal 2025

This portal will provide access and verification of the following:
  • Access to complete and submit the Supplemental Application
  • Status of completed application (all materials received)
  • Receipt of letters of recommendation
  • Receipt of MCAT results
  • Access to pay fee
  • Access to interview information
  • Notification of decision status for different stages
  • Additional information regarding events and enrollment

Application Requirements

Verified AMCAS application
Supplemental application submission
MCAT results
Payment of application fee (or AMCAS fee waiver)
Receipt of letters of recommendation

Important Information

  • Changes to letters of recommendation and personal contact information must be submitted directly to AMCAS and not through the Stanford MedAdmit Portal.
  • Changes to the Supplemental Application will NOT be accepted once the application has been submitted.
  • Information about the review process and application decisions are communicated primarily via email. Please monitor your spam/junk folders.
  • Applicants will receive a confirmation email upon successful submission of the application. If you do not receive the email, review that you have completed every page and selected the Submission button.
  • For information about the admissions process timeline, please visit our website.
