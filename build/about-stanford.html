Land Acknowledgement

Stanford University sits on the ancestral land of the Muwekma Ohlone Tribe. This land was and continues to be of great importance of the Ohlone people. Consistent with our values of community and inclusion, we have a responsibility to acknowledge, honor, and make visible the University's relationship to Native peoples.
