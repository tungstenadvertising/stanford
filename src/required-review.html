Internet Connection

A strong, stable internet connection is critical for participating in Kira Interviews.

Using the same computer and connection, ensure you're able to participate in a Zoom call with your camera and microphone turned on.

Stop any downloads, file sharing, or streaming services on your network to maximize the bandwidth available for Kira.
