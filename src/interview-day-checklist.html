Interview Day
Checklist

1
Upload a photo of yourself (Required)
Upload your name pronunciation (Optional, but encouraged)
Agree to the confidentiality and virtual interview protocols (Required)
Accommodations Attestation (If needed)
2
Confirm Interview Attendance and Register:

Confirm your intent to interview by completing the registration on Zoom (link below) (available ~1 week prior to the interview date — please register as soon as possible and no later than 3 days prior to the interview day).

Your Zoom link will appear below:

Link

Complete the Kira Talent Check in Process:

Log in to the Kira Talent platform to complete the check in process and ensure your computer software and hardware meet the technical requirements. Please test your computer setup upon receiving your unique Interview Day link from Kira Talent. This link is sent the Thursday preceding the week of your interview date from noreply@kiratalent.com. Your unique Kira Link will also populate below:

Link
4
Items needed on Interview Day:
  • AMCAS ID Number
  • Driver's license, state identification, passport, or other photo ID
  • Both the Zoom and Kira  Talent links
5
Student Welcome Reception:

The Stanford Medical Student Association (SMSA) Welcome Reception information will be emailed to you separately by our Stanford Medical Student Recruitment Co-Chairs

Review these resources prior to the interview date:
