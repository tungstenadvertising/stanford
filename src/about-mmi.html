Your Interview

Your Stanford Medicine interview day will be a combination of the Multiple Mini Interview (MMI) and the the Traditional Interview the components. The day will begin with an orientation session and end with an afternoon of presentations about the curriculum and financial aid.

The entire interview day is virtual so you will need a reliable internet connection and a webcam-enabled computer to be able to participate.

On your interview day:

  • You will first log into Zoom for an orientation session (~ 1 hour)
  • After the orientation, you will transition to our virtual interview platform (Kira Talent) for the interview portion (~ 2 hours).
  • Zoom and Kira information are shared via email and will also be available for you in the MedAdmit portal. During the interview portion, you will complete 7 different MMI stations (~ 10 mins each) and a traditional interview (~ 19 mins).
  • At the end of the interviews, you will have a break (~ 45 minutes)
  • After the break, you will log back into Zoom for the afternoon presentations which will mark the end of your interview day (~ 1 hour)
